About Kuea.bali

Established in 2020 and based in Bali, Kuea.bali was born from our love for the art of cake-making — where every detail, flavour, and design tells a story. Inspired by the island’s charm, we create both traditional and modern cakes using natural ingredients, free from preservatives. We specialise in custom birthday cakes, hand-painted to reflect your unique theme or design. Our mission is simple: to bring joy with every sweet bite.
